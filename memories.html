Memories

    Happy Birthday to the girl who knows way too many of my secrets for me to ever let us stop being friends! I’m so glad I found someone whose level of crazy matches mine perfectly. Here’s to another year of bad decisions, late-night laughs, and making memories we probably shouldn't tell anyone about. Let’s celebrate!

    I wanted to write down all our best memories… but some things just don’t fit into words.

    So here are a few moments that stayed with me.

  • Remember that night when we talked almost the entire night? It wasn’t just a late chat. It was one of the best moments of my life… because I spoke in a way I never had before. That night, I discovered a version of myself I didn’t even know existed.
  • And recently, ig on the night of May 2… when I was upset and you were trying your best to make me feel better. Moments like that are rare for me. That’s why it stayed with me.
  • Talking to you on call has become one of my favorite things. I used to hate calls, but with you it just feels right.
  • I like how sometimes you try to keep me around when I’m about to leave the chat at night. You might deny it, but it’s obvious… and honestly, it’s kind of cute. So yeah, keep doing that.
  • Even though we don’t talk as much as before, the energy hasn’t changed. It’s still the best part of my day when I get to talk to you.

Some memories don’t need explanations… they just stay.
