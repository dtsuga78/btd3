Happy Birthday, Idiot Bestie ❤️

You know… you’re actually kind of annoying.

Like seriously, you show up, take over my mood, mess with my head, and then act like it’s nothing.

One minute we’re arguing over something stupid, the next we’re laughing like idiots… and somehow, I never get tired of it.

It’s unfair, honestly.

You weren’t supposed to become this important.

But here you are — stuck in my thoughts, in my routine, in all the small moments I didn’t even realize mattered until you were part of them.

And yeah… I won’t say this out loud because you’d get way too proud of yourself.

But if I’m being real?

Life got a little better the moment you walked into it.

So don’t let it get to your head.

…but also don’t go anywhere.

No matter what happens, our bond will always stay special. Happy Birthday to my favorite human ❤️
